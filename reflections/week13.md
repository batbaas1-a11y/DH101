# Week 13 Reflection

Reflect 13: Do we imagine AI futures as utopian, dystopian, or something in between? What do those visions reveal about us?

I imagine AI futures as somewhere in between—neither fully utopian nor dystopian. AI has the potential to improve healthcare, education, and efficiency, but it also raises concerns about inequality, environmental costs, and control. The outcome depends on how humans choose to design, regulate, and use these technologies. These mixed visions reveal that today we are both hopeful and anxious. We want innovation and progress, but we also fear losing autonomy, jobs, or ethical boundaries. Ultimately, our ideas about AI futures reflect our current values, priorities, and the kind of society we are trying (or struggling) to build.
