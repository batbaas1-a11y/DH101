# Week 9 Reflection

Reflect 9: What is creativity when AI can generate endlessly? Where is the human in generative work?

Creativity with AI is more about choosing and shaping ideas rather than creating everything from scratch. Humans are still important because we decide what to keep and what matters. When I built the bot, I felt like I was guiding it, but not fully controlling it. The AI gave unexpected results, and I had to adjust them, so creativity came from my choices.
