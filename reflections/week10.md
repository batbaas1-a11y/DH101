# Week 10 Reflection

Reflect 10: How does AI change our relationship to play, rules, and narrative?

AI shifts us from being players within systems to participants in systems that evolve with us. It blurs boundaries between player and creator, rule and improvisation, story and simulation. At the same time, it introduces tension: while AI offers more freedom and creativity, it can also reduce transparency, making it harder to understand how outcomes are shaped.
