## Process Notes
I created this project using ChatGPT as a text generator. I designed a prompt that would produce surreal life advice that is both absurd and meaningful. The goal was to create outputs that sounded like real advice but included strange and unexpected elements.

I controlled the tone by making sure the prompt emphasized humor, structure, and slight meaning. By repeating the prompt multiple times, I generated over 50 outputs. Many of them were random or less interesting, while others stood out as more memorable or insightful.

This process showed how quickly large amounts of generated content can become repetitive. Even though the generator can produce endless variations, only a small number felt creative or meaningful. This made the curation process very important.

---

## Reflection (Artist Statement)
This project made me think about what creativity means when AI can generate endless content. While the generator can produce many ideas, most of them do not feel meaningful. The human role becomes deciding which outputs are worth keeping.

The most creative part of this project was not generating the content, but selecting and interpreting it. I chose my best outputs based on humor, emotional impact, and how memorable they were.

This also showed the idea of “exhaustion of novelty.” Even though the generator can create infinite results, they start to feel similar over time. This suggests that creativity is not just about producing ideas, but about recognizing value and meaning in them.
