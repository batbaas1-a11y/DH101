# Week 2 Reflection

Reflect 2: Can AI help us define what it means to be human, or does it reduce us to patterns?

AI can reveal the patterns in how we think and act, which helps us better understand ourselves—but it can’t capture the full depth of human experience. What it leaves out—emotion, embodiment, responsibility, and meaning—highlights what makes us human. Rather than reducing us to patterns, AI sharpens the question of humanity by showing where we go beyond them.
