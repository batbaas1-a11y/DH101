# About Me

I’m someone who enjoys a mix of active and creative hobbies. I love dancing—it’s one of my favorite ways to relax and express myself. Whether it’s just for fun or learning something new, it always puts me in a good mood. I’m also really into soccer, both watching and playing, because I enjoy the energy and competitiveness of it.

Outside of that, I like spending time with people I care about, especially as an older sister—it’s a role that means a lot to me. I also enjoy simple things like listening to music, exploring new interests, and just taking a break from busy days.

I’d say I’m someone who likes to stay balanced: a little bit active, a little bit creative, and always open to trying new things.
