# About Me

This portfolio shares my work from DH101, where I explored how digital tools, AI, data, and storytelling shape the way we understand culture and knowledge.

I build projects that connect critical thinking with creative making. I document my decisions, the tools I used, and the ethical questions that matter most in AI and digital humanities.

## What I care about

- Transparency in AI use and authorship.
- Accessible design and clear writing.
- Meaningful work that reflects cultural impact.
- Learning from both data and narrative.
