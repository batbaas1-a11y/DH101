# How I Use AI

I used AI as a tool to support my thinking rather than replace it. Throughout all the projects, I used AI to help generate initial ideas, clarify concepts, and troubleshoot technical issues. For example, when I was unsure how to structure my visualization or webpage, I asked AI for suggestions and then adapted those ideas to fit my own goals. I also used AI to help debug code and understand how to implement certain features, which made the process more efficient.

However, I made sure that the final decisions, design choices, and interpretations were my own. I critically evaluated AI-generated responses instead of accepting them immediately, especially when working with data and analysis. AI helped me work faster and explore more possibilities, but I remained responsible for ensuring accuracy and meaning in my work.

Overall, AI functioned as a collaborator that supported my creativity and learning, rather than doing the work for me. It allowed me to focus more on understanding the concepts and presenting them clearly.
