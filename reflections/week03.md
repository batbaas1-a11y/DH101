# Week 3 Reflection

Reflect 3: What does “authenticity” mean when identity is co-constructed with AI?

When identity is co-constructed with AI, authenticity becomes less about being purely original and more about intentional choice. AI reflects patterns rather than lived experience, so what feels authentic comes from how a person directs, edits, and intervenes in the process. The output may not fully represent the self, but it can still be authentic if it honestly captures what the person chooses to reveal or question.
