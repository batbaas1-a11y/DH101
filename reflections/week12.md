# Week 12 Reflection

Reflect 12: Is AI sustainable? What ecological trade-offs are we willing to accept?

AI is not fully sustainable right now because it requires large amounts of energy, water, and raw materials to train models and run data centers. This creates ecological trade-offs: while AI can improve efficiency and solve problems like climate modeling or energy optimization, it also increases carbon emissions and resource consumption. The key question is whether the benefits, such as innovation, convenience, and economic growth, justify these environmental costs. Moving forward, sustainability will depend on cleaner energy sources, more efficient systems, and conscious limits on how much AI we choose to use.
