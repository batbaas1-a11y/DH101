# Week 11 Reflection

Reflect 11: Who does the invisible work of AI, and who profits from it?

AI may seem automated, but it relies on hidden human labor, such as content moderators, data labelers, and factory workers. These workers often face low pay and difficult conditions, yet their role is rarely recognized. Meanwhile, most of the profits go to large tech companies and investors. This highlights how AI is not just about technology, but also about inequality and who benefits from the system.
