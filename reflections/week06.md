# Week 6 Reflection

Reflect 6: What do we gain/lose when machines “read” literature for us?

When machines “read” literature, we gain speed and quick patterns. They can show common words, themes, and summaries fast. This helps us see things we might miss. But we lose a deeper understanding. Machines don’t feel emotions or fully understand meaning and context. Literature needs human thinking and interpretation, so machines should help us—not replace us.
