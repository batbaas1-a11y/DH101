# Week 10 – Games & Play

## The Artifact
This project is an interactive narrative game built in Inky that explores how AI influences everyday decisions, relationships, and personal identity. Through branching choices and multiple endings, it shows that while AI can increase efficiency, overreliance on it can reduce autonomy and sense of self.
[Play / View my Inky story](assets/ADayWithoutThinking.ink)

## Process Notes
How did you make this? 
- I created my game using Inky to build a branching narrative structure. I used ChatGPT to help brainstorm ideas, expand the storyline, and improve dialogue and transitions. After writing all the passages first, I connected them using choices and pathways to create different outcomes.
What tools did you use?
- I used Inky and ChatGPT.
What decisions did you make? 
- One major decision I made was to focus on how AI subtly influences everyday choices rather than making it obviously dystopian. I designed the story so that the player slowly becomes more dependent on AI without realizing it. I also chose to include multiple endings to show different consequences, which makes the player reflect on their decisions. Additionally, I structured the narrative so that each choice feels small but builds toward a larger loss (or regain) of autonomy.

## Reflection
This project explores what creativity and decision-making look like in a world where AI is constantly present. Instead of focusing only on AI as a tool, my game examines how it shapes human behavior, identity, and autonomy over time. The branching narrative allowed me to show that small, everyday choices—like asking AI for help or relying on it for decisions—can gradually lead to larger consequences.

The creative process involved both writing and structuring. While I used AI to help generate ideas and refine parts of the story, I made all the key decisions about the direction, tone, and meaning of the game. I decided how the choices connect, what each ending represents, and how the player experiences the progression from independence to dependence. This shows that even when AI contributes to the content, the human still plays a critical role in shaping the final outcome.

One of the most interesting parts of this project was realizing how easily the story could expand. There are almost endless possibilities for branching paths, which made it tempting to keep adding more. However, I had to decide when to stop and focus on making the existing paths meaningful rather than just increasing complexity. This reflects the idea of “exhaustion of novelty,” where having infinite possibilities does not necessarily improve the quality of the work.

Ultimately, this project suggests that creativity is not just about generating content, but about making intentional choices. The AI can help produce ideas, but the meaning comes from how those ideas are selected, structured, and presented. The human role remains essential in deciding what matters.
