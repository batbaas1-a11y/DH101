# AI & Humanity

I use AI as a partner for exploration, not a replacement for my own thinking. In DH101, I used AI tools to generate initial ideas, clarify concepts, and troubleshoot technical problems. At every step, I evaluated AI recommendations critically and chose the direction that fit my own voice and values.

## Ethical considerations

- AI should be transparent: I document what tools I used and how those tools shaped my work.
- AI is not authorship: I make final decisions about meaning, tone, and interpretation.
- AI has limits: I pay attention to bias, missing context, and the social effects of automation.
- AI impacts people and ecology: I consider labor, access, and environmental cost in every project.

## What this portfolio shows

This site is a record of making with AI and making about AI. It highlights:

- how AI can help me think through ideas,
- how digital projects can be shaped by human ethics,
- and why creative work still needs deliberate choice.
