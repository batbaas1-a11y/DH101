## Reflection (Artist Statement)
This project explores the hidden labor behind AI systems, focusing specifically on content moderation and the psychological and emotional toll it takes on workers. While many people interact with AI-generated or filtered content every day, the human effort required to maintain these systems is often invisible. Through my infographic, I wanted to bring attention to the individuals who are exposed to harmful, disturbing, and emotionally taxing material in order to keep digital platforms “safe” for users.

As shown in my infographic , content moderators play a crucial role in shaping online spaces, yet their work is undervalued and often outsourced to regions with lower wages and fewer protections. I chose a clean but slightly dark visual style to reflect this contrast: AI appears sleek and efficient on the surface, but underneath lies a much more complex and troubling reality. The use of images and structured sections was intentional, helping viewers quickly understand the scale of the issue while still engaging emotionally with the topic.

This project changed how I think about AI. Before, I saw AI as mostly automated and efficient, but now I understand that it relies heavily on human labor that is often hidden. It also made me think about ethics—who benefits from AI systems, and who bears the cost? Overall, my goal was not just to inform, but to make viewers question the systems they use daily and consider the human impact behind them.
