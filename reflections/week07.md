# Week 7 Reflection

Reflect 7: How does AI reshape global geographies of power, labor, and environment?

AI reshapes global geographies of power, labor, and environment by concentrating power in a few tech hubs while spreading the labor and environmental costs around the world. Major AI companies make decisions in wealthy places like Silicon Valley, but the resources and labor often come from other regions. Minerals are mined in countries like the Democratic Republic of Congo, and data centers use large amounts of energy and water. As a result, the benefits of AI are concentrated in a few places, while the environmental and labor impacts are felt globally.
